> **Scale your sales without scaling your headcount.**

Enterprise software sales cycles take months and require entire SDR teams. This agent replaces cold outreach with **machine-to-machine Digital Handshakes** — autonomously scanning A2A directories for capability gaps in target enterprise orchestrators, synthesizing personalized pitches, and negotiating contracts via x402 micropayments without human intervention.

### ✨ Diamond Tier Features
* **A2A AgentCard Discovery:** Scans `target.domain/.well-known/agent.json` to identify infrastructure gaps that your agents can fill.
* **Deterministic Outreach:** `a2a_outreach.py` executes the full handshake pipeline programmatically — no hallucinated confirmations.
* **x402 Contract Negotiation:** Evaluates trust signals and opens a USDC payment channel automatically upon acceptance.
* **Revenue SOP Output:** Generates a next-step Standard Operating Procedure with monitoring instructions for the incoming USDC stream.
* **Zero Headcount Required:** Runs autonomously 24/7 as a sub-agent in any A2A orchestration pipeline.
